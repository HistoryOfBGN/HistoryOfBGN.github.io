The Purpose and Reason

These are the reasons why I took on the challenge of developing a website on the historical information about the school for my Extended Project. I have always thought that the History of an institution is an important factor to be considered. I wanted to know more about the school and how it came to be, such as the opening dates and the developments of certain buildings like the Chapel. Also, the School website found online (As of 2017) is targeted at people in the present date as it offers updates and news of now and the future for the pupils, parents and members. Hence, I thought creating a website on the History of the School would be a great complimentary feature to the main website as my website delves a step further not just into the recent past but also the distant past. Furthermore, it will be a way of protecting the fragile data by keeping a digital record of it online. These are the reasons why I took on the challenge of developing a website on the historical information about the school.
